About the Experience

A paddle trip on Rönne River is a sustainable and affordable family activity. Our canoes are very stable and almost impossible to capsize as long as you sit down while paddling. We can add extra seats in the canoe so, in addition to two adults, there is room for 1-3 children (depending on size).

Your adventure begins by gathering at our place at 10:00, where our skilled instructors help you choose the right life jacket and paddle. After that, we take a seat in one of our buses for departure to the chosen starting point. You decide if we should go to Gåsahalsen (about 2 hours paddling), Höja (about 3.5 hours of paddling) or Ugglarp (about 5 hours of paddling). See our paddle map

If you take the train to Ängelholm, it's only a 10-15 minute walk to us. If you come by car, there is free parking here. Best for the environment is, of course, if you take the bike or walk.

When you book, you choose what you want to paddle.

Booking Rules for Private Individuals with Transportation

Our customers often book their paddling well in advance to ensure availability. We think that's great, and it facilitates our planning with staff. We know and understand that things don't always go as planned. That's why you can always (at least 24 hours before departure) rebook for another date with us. To take advantage of this opportunity, we charge a symbolic amount (20 SEK). You can order the service when you make your booking. No hassle. That's how it works at Stisses :-)
