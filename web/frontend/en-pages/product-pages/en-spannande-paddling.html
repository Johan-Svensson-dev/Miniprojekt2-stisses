About the Experience

A paddle on Rönne å is a sustainable and affordable family activity. Our canoes are very stable and almost impossible to capsize as long as you sit down while paddling. We can add extra seats to the canoe so that, in addition to two adults, there is room for 1-3 children (depending on size).

Here in Ängelholm, Rönne å winds through a wonderfully beautiful nature. Your paddling tour starts and ends at Stisses in Ängelholm. The water is usually very calm, so it's excellent for paddling in both directions and is therefore suitable for everyone, regardless of previous experience. If you paddle downstream towards the sea, you will have nature that in some places resembles the Amazon River Delta. If you choose upstream, you will see Ängelholm at its most beautiful. See our paddling map.

When you arrive, you will be welcomed by our skilled instructors who will gladly help you with the right size of life jackets and paddles. When booking, you choose what you want to paddle. Read more here about the options.

Booking Rules for Individuals without Transportation

Our customers like to book their paddling well in advance to ensure availability. We think that's great, and it helps our planning with staff. We know and understand that things don't always go as planned. That's why with us, you can always (no later than 2 hours before the start) rebook for another date. To take advantage of that opportunity, we charge a symbolic amount (20 SEK). You order the service when making your booking. No hassle. That's how it works at Stisses :-)
