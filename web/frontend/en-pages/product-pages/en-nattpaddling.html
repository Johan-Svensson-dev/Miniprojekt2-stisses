About the Experience

Now we're talking about a truly cool adventure. We gather at 21:00 and start with food around the campfire.
We equip you with a life jacket, headlamp, self-illuminating paddles, and earpiece. You listen to the guide's unbelievable stories while waiting for the darkness. Is the story of "Skallepär" true? It is said that he sometimes sits completely still on his bridge in the darkness and just stares...
When we are fed and ready, we glide silently through the night darkness, and you will experience a magically beautiful Ängelholm.

If you take the train to Ängelholm, it's only a 10-15 minute walk to us. If you come by car, there is free parking here. The best for the environment is if you take the bike or walk. Your paddling tour starts and ends at Stisses in Ängelholm. See our paddling map. Here at Stisses, we also have nice lawns and good toilets if you want to sleep in your own tent this night.

If you want to paddle with someone, choose a canoe. If you (dare to?) paddle alone, choose a kayak or SUP in the booking.

Booking Rules for Night Paddling

Our customers like to book their paddling well in advance to ensure availability. We think that's great, and it helps our planning with staff. We know and understand that things don't always go as planned. That's why with us, you can always (no later than 24 hours before the start) rebook for another date. To take advantage of that opportunity, we charge a symbolic amount (20 SEK). You order the service when making your booking. No hassle. That's how it works at Stisses :-)
