About the Experience

If you want to do something really fun with your best friends, paddling is an unbeatable option. There's often a lot of laughter when funny memories and stories are recounted during a few stress-free days of spending time together.

If you're coming by train with a lot of luggage, we can pick you up at the station. If you're coming by car, there's free parking at our location.

Your adventure begins with us gathering at 10:00, where our skilled instructors help you choose the right life jacket, paddle, etc. We pack everything we need into one of our buses and then head to Tranarpsbron where the paddling begins. Accommodation is preferably in beautiful Höja. Your paddling adventure concludes at Stisses in Ängelholm.

Booking Rules for Individuals with Transportation

Our customers prefer to book their paddling well in advance to ensure availability. We think that's great, and it facilitates our staff planning. We understand that things don't always go as planned. That's why, with us, you can always reschedule to another date (within 24 hours before the start) for a symbolic fee (20kr). You can order the service when making your booking. No hassle. That's how it works at Stisses :-)
