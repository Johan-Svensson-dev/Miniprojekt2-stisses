About the Experience

If you want to do something really fun with your best friends, paddling is an unbeatable alternative. There is often a lot of laughter when funny memories and stories are recounted when you finally have time to spend a few days without stress and obligations.

If you're traveling by train and have a lot of luggage, we can pick you up at the station. If you're coming by car, there's free parking at our place.

Your adventure begins by gathering at our place at 10:00 where our skilled instructors help you choose the right life jacket, paddle, etc. We pack everything we need into one of our buses and then drive to Klippan where the paddling begins. Overnight stays are conveniently located in beautiful Ugglarp. Your paddling concludes at Stisses in Ängelholm.

Booking Rules for Private Individuals with Transportation

Our customers like to book their paddling well in advance to ensure availability. We think that's great, and it helps our planning with staff. We know and understand that things don't always go as planned. That's why with us, you can always (no later than 24 hours before the start) rebook for another date. To take advantage of that opportunity, we charge a symbolic amount (20 SEK). You order the service when making your booking. No hassle. That's how it works at Stisses :-)
