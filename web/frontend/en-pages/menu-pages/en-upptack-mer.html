Explore More with Stisses

Paddle and Climb

Throw yourself into the high ropes course and zip through the treetops. Upzone has an adventure and activity for everyone. Walking distance from Stisses.

Paddle and Golf

Ängelholm offers great golfing opportunities. Read more here. If you prefer miniature golf, you can find it within walking or cycling distance from Stisses. More info here.

Paddle and Bowl

Anyone can bowl! Ängelholm's bowling alley has 10 bowling lanes and fully accessible facilities. Walking distance from Stisses.

Paddle and Cycle

The Lergöksrundan bike trail winds along the sea through Kronoskogen and within Ängelholm. Start and finish at Stisses.

Paddle and Swim

With a six-kilometer-long sandy beach, it's never far from a dip. Walking or cycling distance from Stisses.

Paddle and Fish

The most popular fishing is for salmon and sea trout, but fishing is good in many places.

Paddle and Shop

In Ängelholm, you'll find small unique shops alongside several larger chains.

Paddle and Eat

In Ängelholm, there are many opportunities for coffee in idyllic courtyards and restaurants with flavors from all corners of the world.

Kungsbygget Adventure Park

Here, active experiences are offered with summer toboggan runs, ziplines, high ropes courses, climbing towers, cycling, and bungee rocket.

Water House

We have wave ball, stream channel, two water slides, jacuzzi, and children's pools for the little visitors. We also have a diving tower and warm training pools.

Hotell Erikslund

Hotell Erikslund is a perfect base to experience all the beauty and delight that both the Kulla Peninsula and the Bjäre Peninsula have to offer.
