Vattnets Hus

Vattnets Hus wants to be a gathering point for fitness and health enthusiasts. The house was inaugurated on February 7, 2017, and is a full-fledged facility for competitions, play, recreation, and training. It includes a competition pool with 10 lanes, two water slides in the adventure part, a Nordic Hamam Spa, two warm training pools, Bistro, and a gym with three group workout rooms, as well as the Bunker, which is a functional gym.
