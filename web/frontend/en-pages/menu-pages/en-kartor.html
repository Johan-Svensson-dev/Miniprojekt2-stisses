Map Ängelholm

A paddle trip on Rönne å is a simple and affordable option for anyone who wants to get close to nature. Glide along at a leisurely pace, enjoy the bird songs, and the beautiful, varied landscape. Here in Ängelholm, Rönne å winds its way through a stunningly beautiful nature without rapids or lifts. It is perfect for paddling in both directions, making it suitable for everyone regardless of previous experience. You can choose to paddle downstream towards the sea (in nature that in some places resembles the Amazon river) or upstream where the river winds through beautiful Ängelholm.

If you want to paddle for more than one day, there are several good overnight accommodations along the river. If you wish to start or finish from a location other than Ängelholm, we can arrange transportation.
